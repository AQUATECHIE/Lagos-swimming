About Lagos Open water

LAGOS OPEN WATER SWIMMING CHAMPION

Back to memory lane , the 1st edition was launched at The Lagos Oriental Hotel V/l in Nov. 2016 and the Event about 1km swim race took place at The Water Regata Arena, Queens drive behind The French Embassy 2016, with about 59 participants (male 38, female 21 ) We have done 2 more editions covering 2km and 4 km before the COVID-19 pandemic, and the last edition was last year covering 5km long distance swimming (December 3rd 2023). ..

Our Focus

In view of the fact that most Our swimming Champions ends their career at the ages of 20yrs- 24yrs, hence. We want to migrate them to an endurance, competitive and Olympic event that we can participate all through our lives. The least kilometer covered at the yearly and invitational local and international championship is between 5km and 10km. But at the Olympics the race is 10km and that is the focus of this event, pls peruse our proposal and comment on this year edition.
